GetAdly ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve our Service:

If you provide business information (like your business name, industry, logos, or other brand details) to tailor your content, we will collect that as well.
  • User Content: We collect the content of any requests or inputs you submit through the Service. This includes text prompts or messages you send to our bot, and any files or images you upload for us to use or edit. We also collect the AI-generated outputs (posts, designs, ads, etc.) we create for you, as part of providing the Service.
  • Usage Data: We automatically collect technical information when you interact with our website or Service. This may include your IP address, device type, browser type, cookies, and usage logs such as the dates and times of access, pages or features used, and other activity on our site. This data helps us understand how the Service is used and improve performance.
  • Third-Party Account Data: If you choose to connect or integrate third-party accounts (for example, connecting your Facebook or Instagram account so we can publish content on your behalf), we will collect certain information from those platforms. This may include your public profile information, your social media account ID, page or account names, and authentication tokens or credentials required to post content. We access and use this information strictly to operate the Service as instructed by you (e.g., to publish posts or ads) and in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing and Improving the Service: We use your personal identifiers and user content to generate social media posts, advertisements, designs, and other marketing content at your request. For example, we feed your inputs (text prompts, images, business info) into our AI systems to create customized marketing content for you. By using the Service, you consent to receive service-related communications.

  • Analytics and Improvements: We may use usage data and aggregated information to understand usage trends and user preferences in order to improve our Service. This includes analyzing technical performance, fixing bugs, and enhancing AI models or features. This data is generally analyzed in a way that does not identify you personally.
  • Compliance and Security: We may use your information to enforce our Terms of Service and usage policies, to prevent fraud, abuse, or misuse of the Service (for example, detecting prohibited content requests), and to ensure the security of our platform. If necessary, we will use and disclose information to comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal processes, or governmental requests.

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies and similar technologies as described in this Privacy Policy.

We do not share your personal information with these platforms beyond what is necessary to publish your content or as required by those platforms' APIs (for example, using an access token to post to your account).
