Getting Started

Setting Up Your Brand

Before creating your first job, you need to set up a brand. This includes:

  • Upload your brand logo (required for best results)
  • Add business information (name, category, website, social media)
  • Define your target audience and price range
  • Set your business location and contact details

[Image: Brand setup screen showing logo upload and business information fields]

Creating Your First Job

  1. Select a brand from the brand switcher
  2. Click "Create job" on the dashboard
  3. Choose a job type that matches your needs
  4. Provide creative direction or upload product images
  5. Select image size (Post 1:1 or Story 9:16)
  6. Click "Generate" and wait for your design

[Image: Create job form showing job type selector and input fields]

Job Types & Modes

Adly offers multiple job types, each optimized for different use cases. Choose the right one for your content needs.

1. Announcement

Perfect for general announcements, promotions, events, or informational posts. You provide creative direction, and Adly generates a custom design.

Requirements:
  • Creative direction text (required)
  • Product images (optional)
Credits: 1 image credit

[Image: Example announcement design showing event promotion]

2. Product Sale

Designed for showcasing products with prices. Ideal for e-commerce, retail promotions, and product launches.

Requirements:
  • Product images (required)
  • Creative direction (optional but recommended)
Credits: 1 image credit

[Image: Example product sale design with product image and price]

3. Create From Image

Upload a base creative image, and Adly will enhance it with your brand elements, text, and styling. Great for repurposing existing designs.

Requirements:
  • Base creative image (required)
  • Creative direction (required)
Credits: 1 image credit

[Image: Before/after showing base image and enhanced design]

4. Before / After

Showcase transformations, improvements, or comparisons. Perfect for beauty, fitness, home improvement, and service businesses.

Requirements:
  • Base creative image showing before/after (required)
  • Creative direction (required)
Credits: 1 image credit

[Image: Example before/after design]

5. Multi Mode (Multiple Products)

Create designs for multiple products in a single job. Each product gets its own design with name, price, and image. Perfect for product catalogs, sales pages, and multi-item promotions.

Requirements:
  • At least 1 product (up to 10 products per job)
  • Each product needs: image, name, price (optional: old price, notes)
Credits: 1 image credit per product
Limitations: Maximum 10 products per job. Always generates in Post (1:1) size.

[Image: Multi-mode interface showing multiple product rows with previews]

💡 Tip: Product names are always displayed on the design. Make sure to provide clear, descriptive product names for best results.

6. Tips / Educational Carousel

Create educational content, tips, or step-by-step guides in a carousel format. Generates multiple slides (3-10) that work together as a cohesive set.

Requirements:
  • Tips content or educational text (required)
  • Number of tips (3-10, default: 5)
Credits: 1 image credit per slide
Limitations: Always generates in Post (1:1) size for carousel compatibility.

[Image: Tips carousel showing multiple educational slides]

7. Video from Image

Transform a static design into an animated video. Great for making your posts more engaging on social media.

Requirements:
  • Approved design from a previous job (required)
  • Video credits available
Credits: 1 video credit
Limitations: Only available for jobs with approved designs. Generates in Reel (9:16) format.

[Image: Video generation interface showing design to video conversion]

8. UGC Video

Generate User-Generated Content style videos featuring a person talking about your product. Creates authentic, engaging video content.

Requirements:
  • Product images (required)
  • Creative direction (optional but recommended)
  • Language and accent selection (optional)
Credits: 1 video credit

[Image: UGC video example showing person with product]

Credits & Limitations

How Credits Work

Credits are consumed each time you generate content. Different types of content require different credit types:

  • Image Credits: Used for design generation (1 credit per design)
  • Text Credits: Used for ad copy generation (1 credit per copy)
  • Video Credits: Used for video generation (1 credit per video)
  • Landing Page Credits: Used for landing page generation (1 credit per page)

Credit Reset

Credits reset monthly on your billing date. Unused credits do not roll over to the next month.

Trial Credits

During your 7-day free trial, you receive a limited number of credits to test all features. After the trial, you'll need an active subscription to continue generating content.

Limitations

  • Multi Mode: Maximum 10 products per job
  • Tips Carousel: 3-10 slides per carousel
  • Image sizes: Post (1:1) or Story/Reel (9:16)
  • Languages: English, Arabic, Hebrew
  • Brand limits vary by plan (see Plans section)

Checking Your Credits

You can view your remaining credits in the header (desktop) or mobile menu. Credits are displayed by type: Images, Videos, Text, and Landing Pages.

Tips & Best Practices

Getting the Best Results

1. Write Clear Creative Direction

Be specific about what you want. Instead of "make it look good," describe:

  • The mood or feeling you want (e.g., "modern and minimalist" or "bold and energetic")
  • Key elements to include (e.g., "include a discount badge" or "show the product prominently")
  • Color preferences (e.g., "use brand colors" or "warm color palette")
  • Text requirements (e.g., "include call-to-action button" or "minimal text")

2. Use High-Quality Product Images

  • Upload clear, well-lit product photos
  • Remove backgrounds when possible for better integration
  • Use consistent image styles across products in Multi Mode
  • Ensure products are centered and clearly visible

3. Complete Your Brand Setup

  • Upload a high-resolution logo (PNG with transparent background preferred)
  • Fill in all business information fields
  • Define your target audience accurately
  • Add social media handles for better context
💡 Tip: The more complete your brand profile, the better Adly can match your brand voice and style in generated content.

4. Multi Mode Best Practices

  • Use clear, descriptive product names (they will appear on the design)
  • Provide prices in the correct currency format
  • Upload one image per product (not multiple images)
  • Review all products before generating to ensure accuracy
  • Product names stay in the same location across all designs for consistency

5. Effective Design Editing

  • Be specific: "Remove the phone number" instead of "change the contact info"
  • Request one change at a time for better results
  • View all variations as thumbnails before approving
  • For Multi Mode edits, changes apply to all products

6. Language Selection

  • Select the language that matches your target audience
  • RTL languages (Arabic, Hebrew) are automatically handled
  • Ad copy generation respects your language selection

7. Managing Your Credits

  • Check your credits before starting large projects
  • Plan Multi Mode jobs carefully (1 credit per product)
  • Use Tips Carousel efficiently (1 credit per slide)
  • Credits reset monthly, so use them before they expire
💡 Tip: Be patient while jobs are processing. Avoid creating duplicate jobs while one is still running.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • ❌ Uploading multiple images for a single product in Multi Mode (use one image per product)
  • ❌ Vague creative direction ("make it better" doesn't help)
  • ❌ Forgetting to upload a logo (required for best results)
  • ❌ Not reviewing product information before generating in Multi Mode
  • ❌ Requesting too many changes in one edit (break it into multiple edits)

Processing Times

Most jobs finish quickly, but processing time depends on system load and complexity.

  • Images: usually 1–2 minutes
  • Videos: usually 2–4 minutes

In peak times and depending on system usage and pressure, this can increase. Be patient and don’t create new jobs. If after 10 minutes the job hasn’t been created, there is a problem. Contact support and try to create another job.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

"Not enough credits" Error

Solution: Check your remaining credits in the header. Upgrade your plan or wait for monthly credit reset. During trial, make sure you haven't exceeded trial limits.

Design Generation Failed

Solution: Check that you've provided all required inputs (creative direction, product images, etc.). Try simplifying your creative direction or regenerating the design.

Logo Not Appearing in Designs

Solution: Make sure you've uploaded a logo in your brand settings. The logo should be in PNG or JPG format. If the logo still doesn't appear, try re-uploading it.

Multi Mode Products Not Generating

Solution: Ensure each product has an image uploaded. Check that product names and prices are filled in. Make sure you haven't exceeded the 10-product limit.

Edit Request Failed

Solution: Be more specific in your edit request. Instead of "change it," say exactly what you want changed (e.g., "remove the phone number" or "change background to blue").

Publishing Failed

Solution: Check that your Facebook/Instagram accounts are properly connected. Ensure you have the necessary permissions. Try disconnecting and reconnecting your accounts.

Need More Help?

If you're still experiencing issues:

  • Check your plan's support level (Community, Priority, VIP, or Dedicated Manager)
  • Contact support through your account dashboard
  • Review your job history for error messages
  • Ensure your account is active and not expired
